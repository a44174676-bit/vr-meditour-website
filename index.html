Founder Message

Hello. I am Sung Young Jung, founder of VR MEDI TOUR & HOME.

VR MEDI TOUR & HOME began to support international patients who need Korean medical services so they do not feel anxious because of language, distance, systems, and hospital selection barriers.

We are not a hospital and do not diagnose, prescribe, or guarantee treatment outcomes. AI is used only to support consultation preparation such as language check, field classification, and missing information review. Final medical decisions are made only by licensed Korean medical institutions.

We aim to build a trust-based platform connecting medical travel, K-beauty, wellness, and travel support centered on Korea and Vietnam. We help patients understand and choose in their own language, and deliver organized consultation information to hospitals with principles of privacy protection, patient consent, transparent cost guidance, and human-centered coordination.
