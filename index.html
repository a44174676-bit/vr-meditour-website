Founder Message

Founder Message

VR MEDI TOUR & HOME was created to help international patients prepare Korean hospital consultations with less fear, less confusion, and better communication.

We do not replace doctors. We do not diagnose. We do not guarantee treatment results or fixed prices.

Our role is to organize your information, prepare communication, and help you connect safely with Korean medical institutions.
